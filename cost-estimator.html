What does care complexity mean?

This describes your health profile, not the treatment. Your doctor will confirm your level at your first visit. If you're not sure, start with Low.

Low

Healthy pregnancy, no chronic conditions, under 35, no previous complications. Most first-time mums start here.

Medium

Age 35+, previous caesarean, raised BMI, mild blood pressure concerns, or anaemia.

High

Diabetes, hypertension, twins, thyroid disease, previous pregnancy complications, or other chronic conditions.
