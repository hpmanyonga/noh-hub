Busamed Modderfontein Private Hospital

Johannesburg
Primary Birth Facility
📍
4 Cransley Crescent, Long Lake, Ext 12, Linbro Park, Modderfontein, 2090
Network One Maternity — 2nd Floor

Busamed Modderfontein is a modern, full-service private hospital in the east of Johannesburg. It is our primary birth facility, home to the EmpowerHer@Busamed midwife-led maternity programme — a collaboration between Network One Maternity and Busamed. The hospital offers advanced surgical theatres, a dedicated Neonatal ICU, and a 24-hour emergency unit staffed by specialist emergency physicians.

Network One at Busamed Modderfontein

Our Johannesburg maternity team is based on the 2nd floor. This is where you will attend your antenatal consultations and give birth.

  • Dedicated midwife-led birthing suite with 1:1 care
  • Obstetrician on call 24 hours for delivery
  • Doula support during labour
  • Paediatrician available for newborn assessment
  • Direct access to NICU if needed
